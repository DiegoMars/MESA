  • Discover your UC: The Discover Your UC (DYUC) webinar series is a collaborative initiative uniting all 10 University of California Transfer Preparation programs.     • Tuesday, July 29th, 2025, starting at 1pm. Register here.
  • Financial Aid Workshop: Join us at our Financial Aid Workshop and get one-on-one help completing your 2025–2026 FAFSA or California Dream Act Application.
    • Tuesday, July 29, 2025, Student Services and Administration Building (SSAB), 1st Floor, Room 106A, from 1:00pm - 4:00pm
  • Cal Poly Pomona Transfer Success Pathway Workshop: The new Transfer Success Pathway (TSP) program is designed to increase access to CPP. If you faced academic, financial, or personal hardships that prevented you from attending CPP, this program could help you get to CPP to earn your degree.

  • LAMC Enrollment Day: Stop by in person at our and get the help with enrolling in Summer & Fall Classes, counseling, financial aid, and more!

Enhance your resume by developing skills in the field you are passionate about! Review the available internships and scholarships down below clicking on each opportunity to view the eligibility requirements and important deadlines. Lots of new opportunities opened - check them out!

  • L'Space NASA Proposal Writing and Evaluation Experience (NPWEE) Academy: The NASA Proposal Writing and Evaluation Experience (NPWEE) Academy is focused on innovation and creativity. Throughout this experience, participants team up as they learn how to identify NASA needs and develop innovative solutions to NASA's pain points.
    • Application Closes on Sunday, August 24, 2025 - Application
  • AI LA’s IDEAS program: AI LA’s IDEAS is a hybrid 10 week long self-paced program for community college students eager to acquire cutting-edge AI skills and explore its applications in diverse fields.
    • Application Closes on Monday, September 8, 2025 - Application
  • JPL Year-Round Internship Program: The JPL Year-Round Internship Program offers part-time and full-time internship opportunities at JPL during the academic year as well as summers to undergraduate and graduate students pursuing degrees in science, technology, engineering or mathematics.
    • Application is year round - Application
  • Chemistry & Biochemistry Summer Research Opportunity: Are you a community college student passionate about science and looking for hands-on research experience this summer? the CISE program at the CSUN Department of Chemistry & Biochemistry is offering a paid opportunity to work with university researchers.
