College Resources

Take advantage of the college resources available to you as a community college student. Explore the tools and support systems designed to help you successfully prepare for your transfer to your desired institution. Please click on the links below to learn more:

  • LAMC Super Saturday Enrollment Day & Open House: Stop by on Saturday, August 23rd for free tacos & shaved ice, music, fun giveaways (while supplies last), and get help enrolling in fall classes.
    • Saturday, August 23, 2025, Student Services and Administration Building (SSAB), from 9:30am - 12:30pm
  • Cal Poly Pomona Transfer Success Pathway (TSP): Cal Poly Pomona is offering the Transfer Success Pathway (TSP) program for all graduating high school students, or GED, and haven't received any college credit since completing high school, or you are a first time freshmen entering a California Community College.
    • Deadline to apply is Sunday, November 30th, 2025. Click HERE for more info, or email email: tsp@cpp.edu.
  • Microsoft Cybersecurity Scholarship Program: The Microsoft Cybersecurity Scholarship Program supports community college degree and industry certificate completion through financial assistance (up to $500) and subsidies for cybersecurity certification exam costs (up to $375).
    • Application is accepted on a rolling basis. Click HERE to apply.
  • Apply for Financial Aid Before September 2 Cal Grant Deadline: The California Community Colleges Chancellor’s Office’s ICanGoToCollege.com campaign and the California Student Aid Commission (CSAC) are joining forces to urge current and prospective community college students to complete a Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) and the California Dream Act Application (CADAA) before the September 2 Cal Grant deadline.
    • For help in apply for FAFSA, or CADAA, register for in-person and virtual Cash for College Workshops HERE to apply.

Scholarship & Internship Opportunities

Enhance your resume by developing skills in the field you are passionate about! Review the available internships and scholarships down below clicking on each opportunity to view the eligibility requirements and important deadlines. Lots of new opportunities opened - check them out!

  • LA Regional Cleantech Career Academy: Want a future in cleantech? The LA Regional Cleantech Career Academy is offering paid learning and mentorship with local business leaders to help you jumpstart a career in one of Los Angeles’ fastest growing industries. Step off the sidelines and claim your role in building a clean, more sustainable world!
    • Application Closes on Friday, August 22, 2025 - Application
  • L'Space NASA Proposal Writing and Evaluation Experience (NPWEE) Academy: The NASA Proposal Writing and Evaluation Experience (NPWEE) Academy is focused on innovation and creativity. Throughout this experience, participants team up as they learn how to identify NASA needs and develop innovative solutions to NASA's pain points.
    • Application Closes on Sunday, August 24, 2025 - Application
  • CTI LAUNCH: CTI LAUNCH runs from summer through spring and complements what students learn in their introductory CS courses. The program helps students build learning strategies, explore how AI relates to how we think and work, and develop confidence through mini-projects they can add to their resume.
    • Application Closes on Monday, September 7, 2025 - Application
  • AI LA’s IDEAS program: AI LA’s IDEAS is a hybrid 10 week long self-paced program for community college students eager to acquire cutting-edge AI skills and explore its applications in diverse fields.
    • Application Closes on Monday, September 8, 2025 - Application
  • JPL Year-Round Internship Program: The JPL Year-Round Internship Program offers part-time and full-time internship opportunities at JPL during the academic year as well as summers to undergraduate and graduate students pursuing degrees in science, technology, engineering or mathematics.
    • Application is year round - Application
  • Chemistry & Biochemistry Summer Research Opportunity: Are you a community college student passionate about science and looking for hands-on research experience this summer? the CISE program at the CSUN Department of Chemistry & Biochemistry is offering a paid opportunity to work with university researchers.
    • Email mesa.program@lamission.edu your transcripts along with your name, student ID number, and a phone number!
